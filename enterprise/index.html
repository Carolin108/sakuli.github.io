What does Support and SLA Time mean?
Support: Sakuli Core and Sakuli add-ons will be supported on a business impact priorisiation. Consulting on Sakuli projects and setup is not included. SLA: Initial reaction time between Mo.-Fr. German Business Hours 08:00 - 17.00

What does Instances mean?
Number of Sakuli installations. As an example: If you use Sakuli in two docker container and additionally installed Sakuli on a Windows VM, you are using three Sakuli instances.
