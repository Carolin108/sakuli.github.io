What it is

Sakuli is an open platform for a variaty of E2E Scenarios including UI-testing, monitoring or any other kind of automation. You can simulate the interaction of a real user with your target systems.

Sakuli 2 has been rewritten from scratch with the following goals in mind:

  • Ready for the future
  • Open and extendible
  • Easy to use
  • Compatibility with Sakuli V1.x

How it works

 Sakuli extends Seleniums technology for webautomation (in real browsers) with the power of Nut.js to emulate any other possible user-interaction, like opening rich-clients, typing and moving the mouse.
